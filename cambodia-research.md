# Cambodia Investment Research - "别人恐惧我贪婪" Contrarian Angle

## Key Data Points (2025-2026)

### Economic Momentum
- Total trade: $65.2B in 2025 (YoY +18%)
- Exports: $31.3B, market access to 169 countries
- FDI: $10B in 2025 (YoY +45%), 630 approved projects
- GDP growth: ~6% projected for 2026
- China is #1 investor (54%+ of total FDI)

### "别人恐惧" - Why Others Fear Cambodia
1. Thailand border instability - bilateral trade declined ~15% in 2025
2. Underdeveloped capital markets - no government bond yield curve
3. Perception as "risky frontier market"
4. Corruption perception (though improving)
5. Limited infrastructure (but rapidly changing)

### "我贪婪" - Contrarian Advantages
1. **Labor Cost**: Minimum wage $200/month (vs Vietnam $400-450, Thailand $350-400) - CHEAPEST in ASEAN
2. **Tax Holidays**: QIP status = up to 9 years CIT exemption + zero VAT + import duty exemptions
3. **100% Foreign Ownership**: Allowed in most sectors (unique in region)
4. **Dollar Economy**: USD is de facto currency - no forex risk
5. **FTA Network**: RCEP + bilateral FTAs with China, Korea, UAE + preferential EU/UK access
6. **Funan Techo Canal**: $1.2B mega-project, 180km waterway to Gulf of Thailand - bypasses Vietnam ports
7. **Digital Asset Regulation**: New crypto/Web3 framework (NBC Prakas Dec 2024) - first mover in ASEAN
8. **Golden Visa**: 10-year residency from $100K real estate investment
9. **Real Estate**: Foreigners can own via strata title (up to 70% of buildings) + trust structures for land
10. **Young Demographics**: Median age ~26, 60%+ under 30
11. **PM Hun Manet**: New generation leadership, Western-educated (West Point), reform-oriented
12. **Industrial Diversification**: Beyond garments into electronics, auto parts, agro-processing, renewable energy

### Mega Infrastructure Projects
- Funan Techo Canal ($1.2B) - trade game changer
- New Phnom Penh-Sihanoukville Expressway

## "别人恐惧我贪婪" Narrative Framework

### 恐惧面 (What scares people away)
1. "东南亚太乱" → 实际：柬埔寨政治稳定，权力交接平稳
2. "基建太差" → 实际：Funan运河、新机场、高速公路正在改变格局
3. "法治不健全" → 实际：新投资法、数字资产监管框架正在建立
4. "只有服装代工" → 实际：已多元化到电子、汽车零部件、新能源

### 贪婪面 (Unique opportunities others miss)
1. 美元经济体 - 全球唯一以美元为主要流通货币的新兴市场
2. 最低劳动力成本 - 东盟最低，仅$200/月
3. 9年免税期 - QIP项目享受最长9年企业所得税全免
4. 100%外资控股 - 大多数行业允许，越南/泰国做不到
5. Web3先行者 - 2024年底出台数字资产监管框架
6. 运河红利 - Funan Techo运河将重塑贸易格局
7. 黄金签证 - $10万起获10年居留权
8. 总理府级别接待 - 真正的政商高层对接
