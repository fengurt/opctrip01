<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Swiss International Style (Modernist)**
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Clarity & Objectivity**: Content is king; the design frame recedes to let the travel imagery and itinerary details shine.
  2. **Grid Systems**: Strict, mathematical grid layouts for itineraries and photo galleries to create a sense of order and reliability.
  3. **Asymmetry**: Use asymmetric layouts to create dynamic tension and interest, avoiding static centering.
  4. **Typography-First**: Large, bold headings used as graphic elements.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  *   **Monochrome Base**: Black and white dominant to represent the "canvas" of the trip.
  *   **Vibrant Accents**: Specific accent colors for each trip (e.g., Swiss Red for Basel, Electric Blue for Fantasy, Royal Gold for London) to evoke the emotion of the destination.
  *   **Reasoning**: Travel is about the destination's color; the interface should be neutral but structured.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  *   **Split-Screen**: Vertical split screens for desktop (Image left, Content right) or horizontal bands.
  *   **Modular Cards**: Itinerary days presented as clean, structured modules.
  *   **No "Hero" Centering**: Titles and key info aligned left or arranged in a typographic cluster.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1.  **Grotesque Typography**: Large, tight-leading sans-serif headlines.
  2.  **Fine Lines**: Hairline dividers between sections and grid items.
  3.  **Oversized Numbers**: Day numbers (01, 02, 03) treated as massive graphic elements.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  *   **Precise Hover States**: Sharp, instant color changes or underline expansions.
  *   **Smooth Scrolling**: Inertia-based scrolling for long itineraries.

  **Animation**:
  *   **Parallax**: Subtle parallax on images against the static grid.
  *   **Reveal**: Content slides up from behind masks.

  **Typography System**:
  *   **Headings**: *Helvetica Now* or *Inter* (Bold/Black), tight tracking.
  *   **Body**: *Inter* or *Roboto* (Regular), comfortable reading height.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Editorial / Magazine Layout**
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Storytelling**: The layout flows like a travel magazine feature, with varied pacing.
  2.  **Whitespace as Luxury**: Generous margins and padding to create a feeling of exclusivity and calm.
  3.  **Overlapping Elements**: Images and text boxes slightly overlapping to break the digital grid and feel more organic.
  4.  **Serif & Sans Hybrid**: Mixing classic elegance with modern utility.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  *   **Earth Tones & Pastels**: Warm beige, soft sage, slate blue, and cream.
  *   **Reasoning**: Evokes the natural and cultural textures of travel (sand, stone, sky, foliage).
  *   **Low Contrast**: Softer blacks (charcoal) for text to reduce eye strain and increase elegance.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  *   **Broken Grid**: Elements placed "off-grid" to look curated.
  *   **Full-Bleed Images**: Interspersed with narrow text columns.
  *   **Sticky Navigation**: Subtle side or bottom navigation that follows the user.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1.  **Drop Caps**: Large initial letters for day descriptions.
  2.  **Image Collages**: Groupings of 2-3 images rather than single carousels.
  3.  **Serif Headings**: Elegant serif fonts for titles.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  *   **Slow Reveal**: Elements fade in and float up as you scroll.
  *   **Image Zoom**: Slow, cinematic zoom on hover.

  **Animation**:
  *   **Staggered Entrance**: Text lines appear one by one.
  *   **Soft Transitions**: Page transitions that dissolve rather than slide.

  **Typography System**:
  *   **Headings**: *Playfair Display* or *Merriweather* (Italic accents).
  *   **Body**: *Lato* or *Source Sans Pro*.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Immersive / Cinematic**
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Visual Dominance**: The interface is secondary to high-resolution, full-screen imagery and video.
  2.  **Dark Mode Default**: To make images pop and create a cinema-like atmosphere.
  3.  **Minimal UI**: Buttons and text are semi-transparent or hidden until needed.
  4.  **Atmospheric**: Use of gradients and blurs to blend content with backgrounds.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  *   **Deep Darks**: Midnight blue, deep forest, charcoal.
  *   **Luminous Highlights**: Neon cyan, amber, or magenta for interactive elements (buttons, links).
  *   **Reasoning**: Travel is an escape; the dark theme focuses the user entirely on the "window" to another world.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  *   **Fullscreen Sections**: Each "Day" or section takes up the full viewport (snap scrolling optional).
  *   **Floating Text**: Text overlays on images with frosted glass (backdrop-blur) effects.
  *   **Horizontal Scroll**: For galleries or timelines within a day.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1.  **Glassmorphism**: Frosted glass cards for text readability over busy backgrounds.
  2.  **Ambient Backgrounds**: Slow-moving gradients or blurred videos behind content.
  3.  **Glow Effects**: Soft outer glows on active elements.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  *   **Fluidity**: Everything feels like it's floating in liquid.
  *   **Cursor Effects**: Custom cursor or spotlight effect.

  **Animation**:
  *   **Ken Burns Effect**: Constant slow movement on all background images.
  *   **Blur-in**: Content focuses from blur to sharp on scroll.

  **Typography System**:
  *   **Headings**: *Montserrat* or *Raleway* (Light/Thin weights, wide tracking).
  *   **Body**: *Quicksand* or *Mulish*.
</idea>
